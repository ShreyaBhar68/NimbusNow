Help & FAQ

Need assistance using NimbusNow? Below are some common questions and helpful tips to guide you through our magical weather forecasting platform.

How do I search for a city's weather?

Simply type the name of the city or zip code into the search bar on the homepage and click the Search button. You'll receive an instant, magical weather update for that location!

What does the hourly forecast show?

The hourly forecast provides temperature, weather conditions, and the chance of rain for each hour of the day, allowing you to plan your day with a mystical precision.

How do I switch between metric and imperial units?

You can easily toggle between metric (°C, km/h) and imperial (°F, mph) units by using the dropdown menu located next to the search bar. NimbusNow will adjust the units instantly!

How can I view more of the hourly forecast?

If the hourly forecast is too long, you’ll see a View More button at the bottom of the list. Click it to reveal the hidden hours, and click again to collapse the view.

Why isn't the map displaying?

If the weather map isn't showing, make sure you have an active internet connection and that the map’s resources are loading correctly. You can also try refreshing the page.

How do I report an issue?

If you're experiencing issues or bugs, feel free to email us with a description of the problem, and we’ll address it with magical swiftness!
